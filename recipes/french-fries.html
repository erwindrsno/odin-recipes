Pizza

Classic cheese pizza

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • 1 lb. pizza dough
  • 3/4 c. marinara sauce
  • 3 oz. mozzarella cheese, coarsely grated
  • 2 tbsp. grated parmesan
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Start with a solid pizza dough
  2. Make the dough
  3. Proof the dough
  4. Prepare the sauce and toppings
  5. Shape the dough
  6. Bake the pizza
  7. Cool the pizza
